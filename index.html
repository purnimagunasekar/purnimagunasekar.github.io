I began my career in enterprise technology — leading data, cloud, and transformation programs at Infosys, Fiserv, and Bank of America. Over 14 years, I built a deep appreciation for structured execution, rigorous systems, and what it takes to scale.

Then I went back to school. At Georgia Tech's MBA program, I immersed myself in entrepreneurship — conducting over 100 customer discovery interviews, co-founding a startup, and learning the lean startup lifecycle from the inside. I discovered what founders need isn't just strategy — it's a thinking partner who has lived both sides of the table.

Today, I work with early-stage and growth-stage founders across AI, SaaS, eCommerce, and fintech to bridge the gap between big vision and disciplined execution. I've advised mid-size companies & startups, helping them build product-market clarity, defensible moats, and GTM momentum.

My edge is rare: I bring AI strategy expertise, enterprise-grade execution frameworks, and authentic founder empathy — all in one engagement.

"From ambiguity to structured strategy to measurable execution — that's the journey I guide every founder through."
